Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you register for CreditSpread.net, we collect your name, email address, and phone number. We use your phone number solely to deliver trade alerts via SMS. Payment information is processed by Stripe and PayPal — we never store your card or bank details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to: deliver trade signal alerts via SMS and email, manage your account and subscription, send weekly performance recaps (if opted in), and communicate important service updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS with 256-bit SSL encryption. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot see your password. Payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant providers (Stripe, PayPal).

4. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. Analytics data is aggregate and anonymized.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us. Upon cancellation, your data is retained for 90 days for billing dispute purposes, then permanently deleted.

6. Contact

Privacy questions: support@creditspread.net
